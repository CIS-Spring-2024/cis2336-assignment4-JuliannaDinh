Biography

Julianna Dinh is the founder and CEO of UH Sugarland Campus Cafeteria. She is responsible for running all facets of the business, including sales. Dinh has a proven executive management track record and over 20 years of cafeteria design experience. In addition to running the campus cafeteria, Dinh is an avid traveller of the world. Food tours are her favorite things to do in a new country. These experiences give her creative ideas on what kinds of foods she adds to her cafeteria menu. She also visits cafeterias around the world to learn how to stucture her cafeteria more efficiently.
